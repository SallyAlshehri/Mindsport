Explore Chess & Mindsport with Us

OUR Story

Our journey into the world of chess education began in 1972 with a simple yet profound idea: to empower individuals through the game of chess. Since then, we've been steadfast in our mission to make quality chess education accessible to all. Every step of the way, we've remained guided by our core values of integrity, innovation, and unwavering commitment to student success.

Why Choose us

01

Expert Instruction by Grandmasters

Benefit from unparalleled expertise with our team of Grandmasters and experienced coaches who bring a wealth of knowledge and strategic insight to every lesson.

02

Personalized Learning Experience

Experience personalized instruction tailored to your skill level, learning style, and goals, ensuring maximum progress and enjoyment at every stage of your chess journey.

03

Comprehensive Curriculum

Access a comprehensive curriculum designed to cover all aspects of chess, from fundamental principles and openings to advanced strategies and endgame techniques, providing a solid foundation for success.

04

Proven Track Record of Success

Join a community of successful chess players who have honed their skills at our academy and gone on to achieve notable victories in local, national, and international competitions.

05

Holistic Skill Development

Beyond chess mastery, our program fosters the development of essential life skills crucial for success in various domains. Through chess, students sharpen their critical thinking, decision-making, concentration, and sportsmanship, preparing them for challenges beyond the board.

06

Supportive Learning Environment

Immerse yourself in a nurturing and encouraging atmosphere where every student is valued and supported in their growth journey. Our academy cultivates a culture of excellence and mutual respect, fostering a positive learning environment conducive to achievement.
